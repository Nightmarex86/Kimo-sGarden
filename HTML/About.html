About us

Welcome to Kimo’s Garden — where delicious bites meet feel-good vibes! 🌿✨ Tucked away in the heart of Kahale, our family-owned spot started as Chef Kimo’s dream: a place where his love for food and the outdoors could come to life. What began three years ago as a small passion project has grown into a cozy escape full of flavor, fun, and friendly faces. Whether you’re here for a quick bite, a chill night out, or just to soak in the green surroundings, Kimo’s Garden is all about homemade goodness and warm, welcoming energy. Come hungry, leave smiling — that’s how we do it! 💚🍔🌿
